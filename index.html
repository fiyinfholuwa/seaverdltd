About Us

At Seaverdy Care Ltd, we provide home care services in and around Coventry, England. A part of the Profad care Agency Franchise. Our care assistants provide expert support to a broad range of clients with various conditions including:


Elderly care live at home and in Extra Care schemes Adults with complex needs like physical or learning disabilities and dementia, Reablement to help people regain independence after a period of illness, injury or hospitalisation We aim to deliver care which improves the lives of our clients and treats them, and their families, with respect and dignity. We believe our services should not only comply with regulatory standards but exceed those wherever possible in delivering care excellence.

OUR SERVICES

Explore unlimited possibilities

Domiciliary Care

We understand that maintaining independence is important, and our team of dedicated caregivers ensures that individuals receive personalized assistance with daily activities, health monitoring, and companionship right in the familiar surroundings of their home.

Live-In Care

Experience the reassurance of around-the-clock care with our live-in care services. Our compassionate caregivers live alongside individuals, providing continuous support for both day-to-day activities and emergencies.

Home Care

Our home care services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. Whether it's assistance with personal hygiene, medication management, or household chores, our caring professionals are here to enhance the quality of life for our clients.

Social Work Activities

Our commitment extends beyond physical care to encompass the emotional and social well-being of the elderly and disabled. Through social work activities without accommodation, we engage individuals in meaningful interactions, recreational activities, and community involvement.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Seaverdy Care Ltd offer in Coventry, England?

Seaverdy Care Ltd provides home care services, including elderly care for those living at home and in Extra Care schemes, support for adults with complex needs (physical or learning disabilities and dementia), and reablement to help individuals regain independence after illness, injury, or hospitalization.

How are caregivers selected and trained at Seaverdy Care Ltd?

We ensure the quality of our caregivers through a rigorous selection process, including background checks and certifications. Our caregivers undergo comprehensive training programs to provide expert support tailored to the diverse needs of our clients.

How does Seaverdy Care Ltd match caregivers with clients?

We have a careful matching process that considers factors such as skills, personality, and specific care needs. Our goal is to find the right caregiver-client match to ensure a positive and supportive care experience.

What sets Seaverdy Care Ltd apart from other care agencies?

Seaverdy Care Ltd, as part of the Profad care Agency Franchise, is committed to delivering care that not only complies with regulatory standards but exceeds them wherever possible. Our focus is on improving the lives of our clients and treating them, along with their families, with utmost respect and dignity.

What safety measures does Seaverdy Care Ltd have in place for clients?

We prioritize the safety and security of our clients. Our caregivers are trained in emergency response, and we have implemented various safety protocols. Additionally, we utilize technology and systems to monitor and enhance overall client safety.

How does Seaverdy Care Ltd ensure care excellence?

We believe in delivering care excellence that goes beyond regulatory standards. Our services are designed to improve the lives of our clients, and we are dedicated to treating them with the highest level of care, empathy, and professionalism.
